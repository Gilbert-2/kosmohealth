Draggable Menu in Front of Live Feed
This page demonstrates the draggable menu that appears in front of the live feed canvas.
Instructions
  1. Click "Start Camera" to access your webcam
  2. Click "Show Menu" to display the draggable menu
  3. Drag the menu by its header to move it around
  4. Click the menu items to simulate different actions
  5. Click "Simulate Face Processing" to add a canvas overlay
  6. Click "Simulate Gesture Recognition" to add another canvas overlay
  7. Verify that the menu stays in front of all canvas elements
